Privacy Policy & Terms

This page explains how TheoMedia handles website enquiries, project data, cookies, service terms and ownership. It is written as a practical summary and does not replace legal advice for your own business.

1. Who We Are

TheoMedia provides website design, web development, business software, automation and mobile app services for businesses in the UK, Ireland and Europe.

Contact: hello@theomedia.co.uk or WhatsApp/phone +353 85 225 8004.

2. Information We Collect

When you contact TheoMedia, we may collect the information you choose to provide, such as your name, organisation, email address, phone number, project brief, budget range and existing website or software details.

  • We use enquiry information to reply to you and prepare proposals.
  • We do not sell your enquiry details to third parties.
  • We may keep project communication records where needed for delivery, accounting or dispute handling.

3. Cookies and Analytics

The website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand traffic, improve performance and monitor form or page behaviour. If optional analytics or marketing cookies are added, the site should present an appropriate notice or consent option where required.

4. Project Data and Data Processing

For custom software projects, the data responsibilities depend on the agreed scope, hosting setup and tools involved. In most client projects, the client remains responsible for deciding what personal data is collected and why. TheoMedia may act as a service provider or processor when building, maintaining or supporting the system.

Where a project handles personal data, the proposal or separate agreement should define data access, hosting, backups, security measures, retention and deletion responsibilities.

5. Service Terms

  • Project scope, milestones, pricing and payment dates are confirmed in writing before work begins.
  • Timelines depend on client feedback, content supply, third-party approvals and integration access.
  • Third-party services such as hosting, domains, Stripe, WhatsApp, email platforms and app stores are governed by their own terms and fees.
  • Changes outside the agreed scope may require a separate estimate.

6. IP Ownership and Handover

Unless agreed otherwise in writing, the client receives ownership of the custom project code and design deliverables created specifically for them once the final invoice has been paid. Open-source packages, third-party tools, fonts, plugins, stock assets and platform services remain subject to their own licences.

7. Support, Warranty and Liability

Launch support and any ongoing care plan should be defined in the project proposal. TheoMedia aims to build reliable systems, but no website or software can be guaranteed to be uninterrupted, error-free or permanently compatible with every third-party service.

8. Updates

This page may be updated as services, tools or legal requirements change. The current version applies from the date it is published on this website.
